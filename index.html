Welcome to Alma

What We Do

We are building a bot platform that provides a trustworthy, helpful and comforting presence to pregnant women through their pregnancy, by engaging then in a series of highly relevant, tailorable dialogs related to critical topics in maternity care, behavioral health and general wellness.

Values

We believe that healthcare engagement is based first on the simple acts of listening to patients and responding to their individual needs and concerns.

Mission

We create mobile health experiences for patients that engage them on their own terms, treat them as unique individuals, and leave them more informed, activated and empowered.
